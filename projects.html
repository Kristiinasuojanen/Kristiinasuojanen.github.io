Solar energy campaign and website

Together with two fellow students, we designed and created a societal campaign about solar energy.

The project has relevant content, a video, social media platforms, and the main source of informaiton is the website. We created different types of content, accordingly to what we analyzed would benefit the campaign most.

Additionally, we programmed the website from scratch. We used HTML, CSS and JavaScript as languages. Before starting to code, we carefully designed how the site would work and look using figma.

IEM business plan for a restaurant

Me and 3 fellow students made a business plan for a burger restaurant in Otaniemi.

The project required bussiness-minded thinking and knowledge of IEM-terms and principles. Our idea was to start a hangout place for students where they could enjoy delicious burgers. For the project we got a perfect 25/25 grading.
